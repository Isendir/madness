Dancing on a thin line 2012-03-18 02:30 Fiction The Pilgrim

Dancing on a thin line
Life and Death on my sides
Below me: the present time

Dancing on a thin line
A hop, a jump: the crowd cheers as I land
Lady Luck gave me another hand

Dancing on a thin line, I see
An angel, a fairy, the Beauty herself,
Her fall into the fiery pits of Hell

Dancing on a thin line
A wrong step, I stumble and fall
Will I be welcomed to the heavenly hall?

Dancing on a thin line, I saw
The most beautiful girl of them all.
I watched her shine, I watched her fall,
Her body lying on the floor.
Dancing on a thin line, I saw
The gentle instruments of fate,
A man overcome by jealousy and hate

Dancing a on a thin line, we saw
