The mouse behind the keyboard

I don't really like talking about myself, I find it better to ask the Great Duck what it knows about me. Quite a lot, actually: I have my dirty fingerprints on all kinds of strange stuff.

But they all have one thing in common: they're free software. Ever since I first installed GNU/Linux, back in 1998, I've been a fan of free software, regardless of its license (though, for my own works, I prefer the GPL).

But alas, you wanted to know who I am, since you visited this page. Didn't you?

Allright! If the Great Duck wasn't telling enough, know that I am a hacker, or a tiny mouse behind the keyboard. Offline, I'm known as Gergely Nagy (but please, unless you're hungarian, don't try to pronounce my name; if we ever meet, stick with algernon, thank you), and I am a software engineer at BalaBit, working on free software.
